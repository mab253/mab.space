For the austral summer season of 2024-2025, I was the Antarctic Artist and Writers Fellow via NSF/Polar STEAM. I spent 55 days at sea on the R/V Nathaniel B. Palmer, collaborating on a research project studying phytoplankton dynamics in the Southern Ocean, and gathering material to create new media work, an archive of an expedition and a changing climate ...

I am only beginning to process and experiment with the material from this experience. To see some work-in-progress, check out this performance with livecode.nyc in Brooklyn, my public photo updates from the journey, and this interview with the folks at Polar STEAM.
